The Best Movies According to Adrian

My top 3 movies of all-time.

schindler's list

an epic historical drama film directed and produced by Steven Spielberg. This movie reminds me of how quickly humanity can be turned against itself if led by the wrong man. I like to watch the horrific occurrences and the pain and suffering people endured during world war II because it makes me vigilant.

Mad Max: fury road

Directed by George Miller, this post-apocalyptic masterpiece is filled with breathtaking action scenes. you can watch this movie without understanding the story but still enjoy the crazy dystopian world depicted in the movie.

The wolf of wall street

Based on the true story of Jordan Belfort, from his rise to a wealthy stockbroker living the high life to his fall involving crime, corruption, and the federal government. arguably one of my favorite of all time.
